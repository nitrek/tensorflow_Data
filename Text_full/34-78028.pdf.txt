Order Appointing
Tax Administrator

By order dated February 2, 2016, the Commission issued the “Omnibus Order
Directing the Appointment of Tax Administrator in Administrative Proceedings that
Establish Distribution Funds” (“Omnibus Order”), Rel. No. 34-77016, authorizing the
Secretary to issue orders during calendar years 2016-2018 appointing, upon request by
the Commission staff, Damasco & Associates LLP (“Damasco”), a certified public
accounting firm located in Half Moon Bay, California, as tax administrator (“Tax
Administrator”) in administrative proceedings where the distribution fund may incur
tax-related obligations as a Qualified Settlement Fund (“QSF”) under the Department of
the Treasury Regulation § 1.468B-1(c).
On June 9, 2016, the Commission staff requested, pursuant to the Omnibus
Order, the appointment of Damasco as the Tax Administrator for the QSF in the
above-referenced proceeding.
Accordingly,
IT IS ORDERED that Damasco, pursuant to and in accordance with the Omnibus
Order, is appointed the Tax Administrator for the QSF in the above-referenced
proceeding.
