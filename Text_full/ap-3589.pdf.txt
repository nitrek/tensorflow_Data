SALVATORE RENALDI

On January 27, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondent. A hearing in this matter is currently scheduled
for February 29, 2016. On February 9, the Division of Enforcement submitted a motion to
continue hearing and schedule a telephonic prehearing conference.
The Division’s motion is GRANTED IN PART and the hearing is POSTPONED. I
ORDER the Division to notify this office once Respondent has been served with the OIP; I will
schedule a prehearing conference once the Division establishes that Respondent has been served
in accordance with Rule 141(a)(2)(i), 17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(i).
