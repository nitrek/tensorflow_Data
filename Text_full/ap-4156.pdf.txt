ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings (OIP) on August 16, 2016, pursuant to Section 203(f) of the Investment
Advisers Act of 1940. The proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based on United States v.
Jones, 16-cr-27 (M.D. Fla.), in which Respondent Anthony Tyrone Jones, Jr., pleaded guilty to
wire fraud.
The Division of Enforcement advises that Respondent Jones is in the custody of the U.S.
Marshal at a facility in Florida, awaiting sentencing and transfer to a Federal Bureau of Prisons
facility, and that he is available to participate in a telephonic prehearing conference on September
29, 2016, at 10:00 a.m. Accordingly, a telephonic prehearing conference will be held on
September 29, 2016, at 10:00 a.m. EDT.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
