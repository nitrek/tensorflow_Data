ORDER AMENDING OPINION
It is sua sponte ORDERED that the opinion of the Commission issued on June 13, 2016,
hereby amended as follows.
On page 19, the sentence beginning <Specifically, they prohibit . . . > is replaced with the
following sentence <Specifically, Exchange Act Rule 10b-5 prohibits “employ[ing] any device,
scheme, or artifice to defraud”; “mak[ing] any untrue statement of a material fact or to omit to
state a material fact necessary in order to make the statements made, in the light of the
circumstances under which they were made, not misleading”; or “engag[ing] in any act, practice,
or course of business which operates or would operate as a fraud or deceit upon any person.”>
The amended opinion will be issued concurrently with this order.
