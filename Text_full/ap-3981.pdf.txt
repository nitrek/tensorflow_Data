On July 12, 2016, this office received a joint motion to stay this proceeding based upon
Respondent Diane W. Lamm’s and the Division of Enforcement’s agreement in principle to a
settlement that will resolve the proceeding on all major terms.
For good cause shown, the joint motion is GRANTED and the proceeding is STAYED as
to Diane W. Lamm, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice 161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R.
§ 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any of the requirements of the
Rule are not met.
