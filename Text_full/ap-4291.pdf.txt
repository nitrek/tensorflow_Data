ORDER RESCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

Yesterday, I issued an order scheduling a prehearing conference in this matter. Telestone
Techs. Corp., Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 4288, 2016 SEC LEXIS 3955 (ALJ Oct. 20,
2016). Counsel for the Division of Enforcement informed my office that he would be
unavailable on the scheduled date. Accordingly, I ORDER that the telephonic prehearing
conference shall instead be held on November 7, 2016, at 3:30 p.m. EST. Any Respondent that
fails to timely file an answer, appear at the prehearing conference, or otherwise defend the
proceeding will be deemed in default and the registration of its securities will be revoked. Order
Instituting Proceedings at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a), .220(f), .221(f).
