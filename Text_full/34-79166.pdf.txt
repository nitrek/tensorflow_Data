In the Matter of

SECOND EXTENSION ORDER

The Division of Enforcement (“Division”) has requested an extension of time until
April 28, 2017 to submit a Proposed Plan of Distribution under Rule 1101(a) of the
Commission’s Rules on Fair Fund and Disgorgement Plans, 17 C.F.R. § 201.1101(a).
On September 30, 2015, the Commission issued an Order Instituting Cease-and-Desist
Proceedings Pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act of 1933 and Section 21C of the
Securities Exchange Act of 1934, Making Findings, and Imposing a Cease-and-Desist Order
(“Order”)1 against Focus Media Holding Limited (“Focus Media”) and Jason Jiang (“Jiang”)
(collectively, “Respondents”). The Order required Respondents to pay a total of
$55,627,865.43 in disgorgement, prejudgment interest, and civil money penalties to the
Commission and created a Fair Fund pursuant to Section 308(a) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of
2002, as amended.
On June 7, 2016, the Commission issued an order extending the Division’s time until
June 30, 2016 to submit a Proposed Plan of Distribution.2 The Division now requests a
