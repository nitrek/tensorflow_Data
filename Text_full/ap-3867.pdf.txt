PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On May 4, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. On May
13, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a declaration of service, establishing that
Respondents were served with the OIP by May 12, 2016, in accordance with 17 C.F.R.
§ 201.141(a)(2)(ii), (iv). As such, Respondents’ answers are due today. OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R.
§§ 201.160, .220(b). If Respondents fail to timely file answers, they will be deemed in default
and the registrations of their securities will be revoked. OIP at 3, 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a),
.220(f).
The hearing currently scheduled for May 26, 2016, is POSTPONED, and a telephonic
prehearing conference shall be held on 9:30 a.m. EDT on Wednesday, June 29, 2016, if the
proceeding is not resolved before then.
