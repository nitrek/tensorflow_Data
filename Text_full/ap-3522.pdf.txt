ORDER POSTPONING
HEARING AND SCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On January 7, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, alleging that
Respondents have securities registered with the Commission and are delinquent in their periodic
filings.
On January 14, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a declaration of service
establishing that Respondents were served with the OIP by January 13, 2016, in accordance with
17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(ii). Accordingly, Respondents’ answers to the OIP are due by January
26, 2016. See OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.160(b), .220(b).
I ORDER that the hearing scheduled for January 28, 2016, is POSTPONED, and a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 2:00 p.m. EST on February 1, 2016. If
Respondents fail to timely file answers, appear at the prehearing conference, or otherwise defend
the proceeding, they will be deemed in default, the proceeding will be determined against them,
and the registrations of their securities will be revoked. OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a),
.220(f), .221(f).
