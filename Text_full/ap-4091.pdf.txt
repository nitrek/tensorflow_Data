STAY ORDER AS TO ROBERT P.
BEDWELL, CPA

Today, the Division of Enforcement and Respondent Robert P. Bedwell, CPA, filed a
joint motion to stay the proceeding for twenty business days based upon their agreement in
principle to a settlement that will resolve the proceeding as to Bedwell on all major terms.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the joint motion is GRANTED IN PART,
and the proceeding is STAYED indefinitely as to Bedwell, subject to compliance with Rule of
Practice 161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2). If the time limits described in Rule 161(c)(2)
cannot be met, the parties must promptly notify this office.
