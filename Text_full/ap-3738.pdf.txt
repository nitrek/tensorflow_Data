ORDER EXTENDING STAY

Since December 4, 2014, this proceeding has been stayed pursuant to a request by the U.S.
Attorney for the Southern District of New York. Navagate, Inc., Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No.
2093, 2014 SEC LEXIS 4667. On March 23, 2016, the U.S. Attorney submitted a status report,
stating that Gregory Rorke, the defendant in the related criminal proceeding, has yet to be sentenced,
and that a yet-unscheduled evidentiary hearing may be required before sentencing. The U.S.
Attorney therefore requests the stay be continued.
I ORDER that the stay shall remain in effect. I FURTHER ORDER that the U.S. Attorney
shall file a written notice, updating the status of the related criminal proceeding, by June 3, 2016.
