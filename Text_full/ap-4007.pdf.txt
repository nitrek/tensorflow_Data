On July 20, 2016, this office received a joint motion to stay this proceeding based upon
Respondents Strategic Consulting Advisors, LLC’s, and David I. Osunkwo’s and the Division of
Enforcement’s agreement in principle to a settlement that will resolve the proceeding on all
major terms, as to them.
For good cause shown, the joint motion is GRANTED and the proceeding is STAYED as
to Strategic Consulting Advisors, LLC, and David I. Osunkwo, subject to compliance with Rule
of Practice 161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if
any of the requirements of the Rule are not met.
