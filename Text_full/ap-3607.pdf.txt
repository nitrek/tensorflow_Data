ORDER FOLLOWING PREHEARING
CONFERENCE

On January 27, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission denied Diane D. Dalmy’s
petition to lift her temporary suspension and ordered a hearing, pursuant to Commission Rule of
Practice 102(e). 17 C.F.R. § 201.102(e). A hearing is scheduled to begin on February 22, 2016.
A telephonic prehearing conference was held today, attended by counsel for Respondent
Diane D. Dalmy and the Office of General Counsel (OGC). During the conference, the parties
stated that this proceeding could be resolved by summary disposition. I granted OGC leave to
file a motion for summary disposition, and accepted the parties’ proposed schedule below:
April 8, 2016:

OGC’s motion for summary disposition is due.

May 18, 2016:

Respondent’s opposition to the motion for summary disposition is due.

June 3, 2016:

OGC’s reply in support of its motion for summary disposition is due.

I ORDER that the hearing scheduled to begin on February 22, 2016, is POSTPONED.
