This proceeding commenced with the issuance of an order instituting proceedings against
Respondents Deven Sellers and Roland Barrera on December 15, 2015. Pursuant to the briefing
schedule proposed by the parties and adopted by me on January 28, 2016, the Division of
Enforcement filed a motion for summary disposition on February 29, and Respondents’
oppositions were due on March 15. Deven Sellers, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3547,
2016 SEC LEXIS 299.
On March 15, 2016, Barrera submitted a motion requesting that he be given seven days to
file a motion for summary disposition. 1 On March 17, the Division submitted a response
indicating that it does not object to Barrera’s motion for an extension. In accordance with 17
C.F.R. § 201.161, Barrera’s motion is GRANTED, and I ORDER the following briefing
schedule:
March 25, 2016:
April 8, 2016:
April 18, 2016:

Barrera’s motion for summary disposition is due.
The Division’s opposition is due.
Barrera’s reply is due.
