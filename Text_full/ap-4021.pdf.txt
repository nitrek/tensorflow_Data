ORDER SETTING PREHEARING
SCHEDULE AND HEARING LOCATION

On February 5, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondents, pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act
of 1933, Sections 4C, 15(b), and 21C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, and Rule 102(e) of
the Commission’s Rules of Practice. A hearing is scheduled to begin on September 12, 2016.
I set the following prehearing schedule:
August 12, 2016:

The parties shall file and exchange exhibit and witness lists, and
exchange (but not file) final pre-marked exhibits.
The parties shall submit electronic copies of exhibit lists in
Microsoft Word or Excel format to alj@sec.gov. Exhibit lists shall
include exhibit numbers, a description of each exhibit, and Batesstamp numbers, if any.

August 26, 2016:

The parties shall file and exchange objections to witnesses and
exhibits, stipulations, and motions in limine, if any.
The parties shall file and exchange expert reports, if any.

September 7, 2016:

A final telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 2:00 p.m.
Eastern.

September 12, 2016: The hearing shall commence in Courtroom 9, U.S. Court of

Federal Claims, 717 Madison Place NW, Washington, D.C. 20439.
Because of extensive summary disposition briefing, the parties need not file prehearing
briefs, but may each present opening statements not exceeding twenty minutes. If the parties
desire to seek relief from the above schedule, they may file a motion.
SO ORDERED.
