CONFERENCE

On February 17, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.
This proceeding has already ended with respect to all respondents but Ceyoniq AG (n/k/a
Ceyoniq, Inc.). See Ceyoniq AG, Initial Decision Release No. 999 (ALJ Apr. 12, 2016).
Ceyoniq AG has not yet been served with the OIP. Accordingly, the telephonic
prehearing conference scheduled for April 14, 2016, is POSTPONED, and a telephonic
prehearing conference shall be held on May 23, 2016, at 11:00 a.m. EDT, if the proceeding has
not been resolved by then.
