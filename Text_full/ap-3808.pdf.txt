PAUL LEON WHITE, II

On April 15, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) against Respondent. A hearing in this matter is currently scheduled for May
16, 2016. On April 25, the Division of Enforcement filed a motion to convert the hearing into a
telephonic prehearing conference.
The Division’s motion is GRANTED IN PART and the hearing is POSTPONED. I
ORDER the Division to notify this office once Respondent has been served with the OIP; I will
schedule a prehearing conference once the Division establishes that Respondent has been served
in accordance with Rule 141(a)(2)(i), 17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(i).
