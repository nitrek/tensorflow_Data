On June 8, 2015, the United States District Court for the Northern District of Georgia
entered an order in Hill v. SEC, No. 1:15-cv-1801, preliminarily enjoining the Securities and
Exchange Commission from proceeding in this matter, prompting the Chief Administrative Law
Judge to cancel the upcoming hearing. Charles L. Hill, Jr., Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No.
2789, 2015 SEC LEXIS 2288 (ALJ June 9, 2015). Following a ruling by the United States Court
of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit, the district court dismissed Hill v. SEC on September 14,
2016. ECF No. 51.
On September 16, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a notice of the district courtâ€™s
dismissal and requested that I schedule a prehearing conference. The parties have represented
that they are available for a telephonic prehearing conference at 2:00 p.m. EDT on September 27,
2016. Accordingly, I ORDER that a telephonic prehearing conference will occur on September
27, 2016, at 2:00 p.m. EDT. Before the conference, the parties should confer and propose a
hearing date.
