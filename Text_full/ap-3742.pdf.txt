CONFERENCE

The Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting proceedings on March 8,
2016, alleging that Geo Global Group Ltd. (f/k/a Geo Genesis Group, Ltd.) has securities registered
with the Commission pursuant to Section 12(g) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and has not
filed required periodic reports since it filed a Form 20-FR12G registration statement on November 19,
2008. Geo Global filed an answer on March 17, 2016, denying that it has securities registered with the
Commission. On March 24, 2016, I postponed the scheduled hearing and ordered a telephonic
prehearing conference to be held at 11:00 a.m. EDT on March 30, 2016. Geo Global Grp. Ltd.,
Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3731, 2016 SEC LEXIS 1109.
Today I received a copy of an email from Geo Globalâ€™s counsel to the Division of
Enforcement, stating that Geo Global wished to settle the proceeding and requesting a proposed offer
of settlement.
I POSTPONE the prehearing conference scheduled for March 30 to Wednesday, April 13 at
11:00 a.m. EDT, at which, if the parties have not agreed in principle to a settlement on all major terms,
we will set a prehearing schedule.
