ORDER POSTPONING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On July 19, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
administrative proceedings (OIP) against Respondents Saving2Retire, LLC, and Marian P.
Young. A prehearing conference is currently scheduled for August 18, 2016, at 3:00 p.m. EDT.
On August 12, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a status report regarding its
efforts to serve and meet and confer with Respondents. See Saving2Retire, LLC, Admin. Proc.
Rulings Release No. 4028, 2016 SEC LEXIS 2617 (ALJ July 28, 2016). The Division reported
that the OIP was sent to Respondents by U.S. Postal Service and United Parcel Service, and that
delivery had been attempted but not yet accomplished. To allow time for service of the OIP on
Respondents in accordance with Rule of Practice 141(a)(2), 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.141(a)(2), the
prehearing conference will be POSTPONED. Once the Division provides proof of service in
accordance with Rule of Practice 141(a)(2), I will reschedule the prehearing conference.
