ORDER GRANTING JOINT MOTION
FOR MODIFICATION OF
PROTECTIVE ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting proceedings on
March 29, 2016. On May 25, 2016, I adopted the procedural schedule agreed upon by the
parties; the hearing will begin on September 12, 2016. Christopher M. Gibson, Admin. Proc.
Rulings Release No. 3866, 2016 SEC LEXIS 1844.
On August 3, 2016, the parties filed a joint motion to modify a stipulated protective order
that I accepted into evidence on April 18, 2016. See Christopher M. Gibson, Admin. Proc.
Rulings Release No. 3788, 2016 SEC LEXIS 1408.
The joint motion is GRANTED, and I ORDER that the stipulated protective order in
place in this proceeding is modified as follows:
1. Paragraph 1, delete the words, “provided further, that SPCI does not include
information or documents Respondent or his counsel obtained other than through
receipt of Investigative Record Materials.”
2. Paragraph 6, insert at the conclusion of the paragraph the following, “For purposes of
this paragraph 6, the term ‘filings’ shall be deemed to include all documents
identified as exhibits by Respondent in this proceeding, and any other documents
ordered by the hearing officer to be produced by the Respondent in connection with
the hearing in this matter.”
