SCHEDULING ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on July 14, 2016, pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act of 1933,
Section 21C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, Sections 203(e) and 203(f) of the
Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and Section 9(b) of the Investment Company Act of 1940. The
hearing is set to commence on March 20, 2017.1 As proposed by the parties, the following
schedule is adopted:
September 30, 2016: Parties exchange preliminary witness lists.
October 11, 2016: Parties identify witnesses whom they expect to call to offer expert testimony.2
January 13, 2017: Conclusion of depositions of fact witnesses.
January 20, 2017: Deadline for expert disclosures required under 17 C.F.R. § 201.222(b).
February 17, 2017: Deadline for completing expert depositions.
March 1, 2017: Parties file final witness and exhibit lists and exchange pre-marked exhibits.
March 8, 2017: Exchange and file prehearing briefs, as well as motions in limine and any
objections to exhibits and witnesses.
March 13, 2017: Final telephonic prehearing conference at 1:00 p.m. EDT.
March 20, 2017: Hearing to begin at a location to be announced.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
/S/ Carol Fox Foelak
