ORDER FOLLOWING PREHEARING
CONFERENCE AND EXTENDING DEADLINE
FOR FILING MOTION FOR SANCTIONS

On October 13, 2015, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against James A. Evans, Jr. On December 28, 2015, I found Evans
in default for failing to answer the OIP or otherwise defend this proceeding, and I directed the
Division of Enforcement to file a motion for sanctions by January 15, 2016. James A. Evans, Jr.,
Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3434, 2015 SEC LEXIS 5301.
A telephonic prehearing conference was held today, at which Division counsel requested
that the deadline for filing a motion for sanctions be extended to February 12, 2016. Consistent
with 17 C.F.R. § 201.161, I granted this request. The Division’s motion for sanctions is now due
on February 12, 2016.
SO ORDERED.
