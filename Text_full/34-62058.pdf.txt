Respondents.

On July 19, 2006, the Commission published a notice of the Plan of Distribution (“Plan”)
proposed by the Division of Enforcement in connection with this proceeding (Exchange Act
Release No. 54175). The Commission received comments and on April 6, 2007 the Plan was
approved. (Exchange Act Release No. 55598).
The Plan provides that a Fair Fund consisting of $140,000,000 in disgorgement and civil
penalties, plus any accrued interest, be transferred to Deutsche Bank to be distributed by the Plan
Administrator to injured investors according to the methodology set forth in the Plan. The Plan
provides that the Commission will arrange for distribution of the Fair Fund when a Payment File
listing the payees with the identification information required to make the distribution has been
received and accepted. The Payment File for the first Holdback distribution, in the amount of
$19,999,962.62, as described in the Plan, ¶ 7.8, has been received and accepted.1
