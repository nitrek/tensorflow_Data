STAY ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on February 12, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b)(6) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940.
The parties jointly requested a stay pending Commission consideration of Respondent’s
offer of settlement, stating that they have agreed in principle to a settlement on all major terms.
Accordingly, the proceeding, including all procedural dates, will be stayed, contingent upon
compliance with 17 C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2).
IT IS SO ORDERED.
