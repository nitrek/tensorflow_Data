ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE

On December 22, 2015, the Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding
pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, alleging that Respondents have
securities registered with the Commission and are delinquent in their periodic filings. A prehearing
conference is scheduled for January 15, 2016.
On January 4, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a service declaration, representing
that Respondents were served with the OIP by December 30, 2015, in accordance with 17 C.F.R. §
201.141(a)(2)(ii), (iv).1 Accordingly, Respondents’ answers were due by January 12, 2016. OIP at
3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a)(2), .220(f). To date, Respondents have failed to file answers.
It is ORDERED that Respondents SHOW CAUSE by January 25, 2016, why this
proceeding should not be determined against them due to their failures to file answers or otherwise
defend this proceeding. If Respondents fail to respond to this order, they will be deemed in default
and the proceeding will be determined against them. See OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a)(2),
.220(f).
The prehearing conference will take place as scheduled on January 15, 2016, at 2:00 p.m.
EST.
