On October 25, 2016, the Division of Enforcement moved for additional time to respond
to Respondent’s motion to correct transcripts, which relates to both the hearing transcript and
portions of the investigative testimony allowed into evidence. The Division notes that the
proposed corrections are numerous and that it has been occupied in completing its post-hearing
brief and proposed findings of fact and conclusions of law, filed on October 24, 2016.
I GRANT the Division’s request. The Division’s response to Respondent’s motion to
correct transcripts is due November 4, 2016.
