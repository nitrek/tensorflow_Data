ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on February 12, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and a hearing
was scheduled to commence on March 7, 2016. The proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based
on SEC v. Schooler, No. 3:12-cv-2164 (S.D. Cal.), in which Respondent Louis V. Schooler was
enjoined against violations of the antifraud and registration provisions.
As the parties request, and pursuant to 17 C.F.R. ยง201.161, the hearing will be postponed
sine die, and a prehearing conference will be held by telephone on March 16, 2016, at 12:30 p.m.
EDT (9:30 a.m. PDT).
IT IS SO ORDERED.
