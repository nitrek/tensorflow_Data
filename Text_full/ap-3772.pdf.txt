ORDER ON POST-INITIAL DECISION
MOTION

On March 23, 2016, I issued the initial decision in this matter. See James S. Tagliaferri,
Initial Decision Release No. 985, 2016 SEC LEXIS 1088. On April 5, 2016, Respondent filed a
“Petition for Review of Court’s Initial Decision Dated March 23, 2016 and Motion to Correct
Manifest Errors of Fact.” In the motion, Respondent “petitions the Court to review its initial
decision” and “moves the Court to correct ‘manifest errors of fact’ contained in [the initial
decision].” Mot. at 1.
To the extent the motion is intended to be a motion to correct a manifest error of fact
pursuant to Rule 111(h), the Division of Enforcement shall have until April 11, 2016, to file an
opposition. See 17 C.F.R. § 201.111(h). Respondent’s reply, if any, is due by April 18, 2016.
To the extent the motion is intended to be a petition for review pursuant to Rule 410, it is
necessarily directed to the Commission. See 17 C.F.R. § 201.410(a), (b).
SO ORDERED.
