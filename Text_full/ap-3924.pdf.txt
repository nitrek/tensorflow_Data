ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings (OIP) on May 12, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. Blount was
served with the OIP, in accordance with 17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(i), by personal service on June
2, 2016.1
As the parties request, a prehearing conference will be held by telephone on June 28,
2016, at 10:00 a.m. EDT (9:00 a.m. CDT).
IT IS SO ORDERED.
