POSTPONEMENT ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on July 14, 2016, pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act of 1933,
Section 21C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, Sections 203(e) and 203(f) of the
Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and Section 9(b) of the Investment Company Act of 1940. A
hearing was scheduled to commence on August 18, 2016.
As the parties request, and pursuant to 17 C.F.R. § 201.161, the hearing will be postponed
sine die, and a prehearing conference will be held by telephone on August 22, 2016, at 1:00 p.m.
EDT (10:00 a.m. PDT).
Recently the Commission amended its rules of practice applicable to administrative
proceedings. Amendments to the Commission’s Rules of Practice, 81 Fed. Reg. 50212 (July 29,
2016). As the parties request, the amended rules will apply to this proceeding. Id. at 50228-29.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
