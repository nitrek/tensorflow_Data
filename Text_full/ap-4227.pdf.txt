ORDER SCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On November 17, 2015, the United States District Court for the Northern District of
Georgia entered an order in Ironridge Global IV, Ltd. v. SEC, No. 1:15-cv-2512, preliminarily
enjoining the Securities and Exchange Commission from proceeding in this matter and
prompting me to cancel the schedule then in place. See Ironridge Glob. Partners, LLC, Admin.
Proc. Rulings Release No. 3329, 2015 SEC LEXIS 4759 (ALJ Nov. 17, 2015). On September
28, 2016, the district court approved plaintiff’s notice of voluntary dismissal without prejudice
pursuant to Federal Rule of Civil Procedure 41(a)(1)(i), thus vacating the injunction.1 See
Ironridge v. SEC, Docket, ECF No. 32.
The parties have notified my office that they are available for a prehearing conference on
October 18 or 19, 2016. Accordingly, I ORDER that a telephonic prehearing conference will
occur on October 19, 2016, at 2:00 p.m. EDT. Before the conference, the parties should confer
and propose a prehearing and hearing schedule.
