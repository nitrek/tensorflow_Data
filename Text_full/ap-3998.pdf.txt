In the Matter of

ORDER RESCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

WILLIAM TIRRELL
On June 23, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
administrative and cease-and-desist proceedings (OIP) against Respondent William Tirrell. A
prehearing conference is scheduled for July 21, 2016.
Yesterday, the parties filed a joint letter asserting that service of the OIP on Mr. Tirrell
will not be effective until September 13, 2016. The letter requests that the prehearing conference
be rescheduled for on or about October 6, 2016.
It is ORDERED that the prehearing conference is RESCHEDULED for October 6, 2016,
at 11:00 a.m. Eastern.
