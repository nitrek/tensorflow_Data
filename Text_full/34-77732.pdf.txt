NOTICE THAT INITIAL DECISION ON EAJA APPLICATION HAS BECOME FINAL
The time for filing a petition for review of the initial decision concerning Thomas R.
Delaney II’s application under the Equal Access to Justice Act (“EAJA”) has expired. No such
petition has been filed by Thomas R. Delaney II and the Commission has not chosen to review
the decision on its own initiative.
Accordingly, notice is hereby given, pursuant to Rule 360(d) of the Commission's Rules
of Practice,1 that the initial decision of the administrative law judge has become the final
decision of the Commission with respect to Thomas R. Delaney II.2 The initial decision denied
Thomas R. Delaney II’s application to recover fees and costs under EAJA.
For the Commission, by the Office of the General Counsel, pursuant to delegated
authority.
