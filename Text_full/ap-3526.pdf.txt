ORDER POSTPONING HEARING
AND SCHEDULING PREHEARING
CONFERENCE

On January 6, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) against Respondent pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities Exchange Act
of 1934. Respondent’s answer to the OIP is due twenty days after service of the OIP on him.
OIP at 2; 17 C.F.R. § 201.220(b).
I POSTPONE the hearing currently scheduled for January 29, 2016, and schedule a
prehearing conference on February 9, 2016, at 2:00 p.m. EST. The parties should be prepared to
discuss a procedural schedule to resolve this proceeding.
The Division of Enforcement shall file a declaration by January 27, 2016, regarding
whether service of the OIP has been accomplished in accordance with Rule of Practice 141, 17
C.F.R. § 201.141, and, if so, provide proof of service.
