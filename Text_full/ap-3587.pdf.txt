ORDER POSTPONING HEARING

CONFERENCE

On February 4, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934,
alleging that Respondents have securities registered with the Commission and are delinquent in
their periodic filings. A hearing is scheduled for February 24, 2016.
On February 9, 2016, the Division of Enforcement submitted a declaration of service,
establishing that Respondents were served with the OIP by February 6, 2016, in accordance with
17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(ii). As such, Respondents’ answers are due by February 19, 2016.
OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.160(b), .220(b).
I ORDER that the hearing currently scheduled for February 24, 2016, is POSTPONED,
and a telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 3:00 p.m. EST on March 8, 2016, if the
proceeding is not resolved before then. Any Respondent that fails to file an answer by February
19, 2016, will be deemed in default, the proceeding will be determined against it, and the
registration of its securities will be revoked. See OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a)(2), .220(f).
