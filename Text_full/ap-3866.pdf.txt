CHRISTOPHER M. GIBSON

The Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting proceedings on March 29,
2016. At a prehearing conference on May 9, 2016, the parties agreed to a start date of September 12,
2016, for the hearing. Christopher M. Gibson, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3834, 2016 SEC
LEXIS 1682, at *2 (ALJ May 10, 2016). I then ordered the parties to file a proposed schedule setting out
prehearing procedural deadlines. Id.
The parties filed a joint motion for entry of a prehearing schedule on May 23, 2016. The motion is
GRANTED, and I adopt the parties’ proposed schedule as follows:
July 8, 2016:

The Division provides Respondent with its witness and exhibit lists, and
copies of pre-marked exhibits.

July 15, 2016:

The Division provides Respondent with its expert report(s).

July 22, 2016:

Respondent provides the Division with his witness and exhibit lists, and
copies of pre-marked exhibits.

July 29, 2016:

Respondent identifies any objections to the Division’s exhibits.

August 5, 2016:

Respondent provides the Division with his expert report(s).

August 12, 2016:

The Division identifies any objections to Respondent’s exhibits.

August 19, 2016:

The Division provides Respondent with its rebuttal expert report(s).

August 26, 2016:

Prehearing briefs are filed.

September 12, 2016: The hearing begins in Washington, D.C., at Commission Headquarters.
Only the prehearing briefs are to be filed with the Office of the Secretary.
