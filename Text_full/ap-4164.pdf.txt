ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on September 1, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934, and a hearing was scheduled to commence on September 28, 2016. The
proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based on SEC v. Milligan, No. 2:15-cv-07308 (C.D. Cal.),
in which Respondent was enjoined against violations of the antifraud and registration provisions.
As the parties request, and pursuant to 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.161, the hearing will be postponed
sine die, and a prehearing conference will be held by telephone on October 12, 2016, at 1:00 p.m.
EDT (12:00 noon CDT, 11:00 a.m. MDT).
IT IS SO ORDERED.
