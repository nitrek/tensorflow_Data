STAY ORDER AS TO CARLTON W.
VOGT, III

On December 31, 2015, this office received a joint motion to stay this proceeding as to
Respondent Carlton W. Vogt, III, CPA, based upon his and the Division of Enforcement’s
agreement in principle to a settlement that will resolve the proceeding as to Vogt on all major
terms.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the joint motion is GRANTED, and the
proceeding is STAYED as to Vogt, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice 161(c)(2), 17
C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any of the requirements
of the Rule are not met.
