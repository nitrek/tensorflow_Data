SCHEDULING ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on May 19, 2016, pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act of 1933,
Section 21C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, Sections 203(e), 203(f), and 203(k) of the
Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and Section 9(b) of the Investment Company Act of 1940.
With the agreement of the parties and consistent with 17 C.F.R. §§ 201.200(c), .222, the
following schedule is adopted:
August 12, 2016: The parties exchange and file witness lists and expert disclosures, including
expert reports.1
September 14, 2016: The parties exchange and file exhibit lists and exchange pre-marked exhibits.
September 21, 2016: The parties file prehearing briefs, any motions in limine, and any objections to
exhibits and witnesses; the parties identify rebuttal experts and exchange any rebuttal expert
disclosures, including expert reports.
September 23, 2016: The parties file any written stipulations.
September 26, 2016: Final prehearing conference at 11:00 a.m. EDT.
October 4, 2016: Hearing commences in St. Louis, MO. Hearing sessions will be held October 47, 13-14.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
/S/ Carol Fox Foelak
Carol Fox Foelak
