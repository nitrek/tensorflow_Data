ORDER DIRECTING
DISBURSEMENT OF FAIR FUND

On March 13, 2008, the Commission published a Notice of the Plan of Distribution
(“Plan”) proposed by the Division of Enforcement in connection with this proceeding (Securities
Exchange Act Release No. 57489). The Commission received no comments and on May 15,
2008, the Plan was approved (Exchange Act Release No. 57825).
The Plan provides that a Fair Fund consisting of disgorgement and civil penalties, plus
any accrued interest, be transferred to Deutsche Bank to be distributed by the Fund
Administrator to injured investors according to the methodology set forth in the Plan.1 To date,
$341,982,093.86 has been disbursed in six tranches.2 The Plan provides that the Commission
