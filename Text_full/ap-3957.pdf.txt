STAY ORDER

Today, this office received a joint motion to stay this proceeding as to Respondent
Dragon Bright Mintai Botanical Technology Cayman Ltd. based upon its agreement in principle
with the Division of Enforcement to a settlement that will resolve the proceeding as to Dragon
Bright on all major terms.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the motion is GRANTED, and the
proceeding is STAYED as to Dragon Bright, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice
161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any of the
requirements of the rule are not met.
