SALVATORE RENALDI

At the March 30, 2016, prehearing conference, the Division of Enforcement notified me
that the parties had reached an agreement in principle on all major terms and Respondent had
signed an offer of settlement. Salvatore Renaldi, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3753, 2016
SEC LEXIS 1203 (ALJ Mar. 31, 2016). On April 29, 2016, the Division filed a joint motion to
stay this proceeding, noting that the signed offer of settlement had been submitted to the
Commission for consideration.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the joint motion is GRANTED, and the
proceeding is STAYED, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice 161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R. ยง
201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any of the requirements of the
Rule are not met.
