ORDER FOLLOWING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

Today, a telephonic prehearing conference was held at which the Division of
Enforcement and counsel for Respondents appeared. I determined that the hearing in this matter
shall occur in Atlanta, Georgia, from February 7 to February 10, 2017, and will continue the
week of February 13, 2017, if necessary. I direct the parties to file by October 11, 2016, a joint
proposed prehearing schedule consistent with what was discussed during the conference.
SO ORDERED.
