ORDER POSTPONING
HEARING AND SCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On September 23, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondent pursuant to Section 21C of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934. A hearing is scheduled for October 24, 2016.
On October 14, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a motion to postpone the hearing
and schedule a prehearing conference, stating that counsel for Respondent has indicated that
Respondent has decided to settle the proceeding.
To allow time for the parties to reach an agreement in principle to a settlement on all
major terms, I GRANT the Division’s motion and POSTPONE the October 24, 2016, hearing. I
ORDER that a telephonic prehearing conference be held on November 21, 2016, at 10:00 a.m.
EST if the parties have not filed a motion to stay the proceeding pursuant to Rule of Practice
161(c)(2), 17 C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2), by then.
