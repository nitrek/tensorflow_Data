CONFERENCE

On October 2, 2015, I denied the Division of Enforcement’s summary disposition
motion. Gary L. McDuff, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3190, 2015 SEC LEXIS 4040, at
*24-25. Subsequently, on January 11, 2016, I denied McDuff’s summary disposition motion.
Gary L. McDuff, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3482, 2016 SEC LEXIS 82, at *5. As
McDuff is currently incarcerated at FCI Beaumont Low, I ordered the Division to submit a letter
describing its discussions with McDuff and facility staff regarding the logistics of holding a
hearing. Id.
Both the Division and McDuff submitted letters regarding hearing logistics. To discuss
this issue, I ORDER that a telephonic prehearing conference be held on February 18, 2016, at
4:00 p.m. EST.
