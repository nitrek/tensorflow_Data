PREHEARING ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on November 9, 2015, pursuant to Sections 15(b) and 21C of the
Securities Exchange Act of 1934. A prehearing conference was held today. Martin F. Healey
and Ellen Moynihan, Esqs., appeared on behalf of the Division of Enforcement, and Respondent
Sandip Shah appeared pro se.
The parties agreed to discuss settlement. A second prehearing conference will be held by
telephone on February 4, 2016, at 12:00 noon EST.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
