ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE

On August 31, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) against Respondents pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act
of 1934.1
I previously found that Respondents were served with the OIP by September 8 and their
answers were due by September 21, 2016. Multi-Corp. Int’l, Inc., Admin. Proc. Rulings Release
No. 4178, 2016 SEC LEXIS 3528 (ALJ Sept. 19, 2016). To date, Respondents have not filed
answers.
Accordingly, Respondents are ORDERED to SHOW CAUSE by October 3, 2016, why
the registrations of their securities should not be revoked by default due to their failures to file
answers or otherwise defend the proceeding. If Respondents fail to respond to this order, they
will be deemed in default and the registrations of their securities will be revoked. OIP at 3; 17
C.F.R. §§ 201.155(a)(2), .220(f).
