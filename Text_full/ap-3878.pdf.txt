ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on May 13, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities Exchange
Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and a hearing was
scheduled to commence on June 7, 2016. The proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based on
SEC v. Benger, No. 1:09-cv-00676 (N.D. Ill.), appeal docketed, No. 16-1886 (7th Cir. Apr. 22,
2016), in which Respondent was enjoined against violations of the antifraud provisions.
As the parties request, and pursuant to 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.161, the hearing will be postponed
sine die, and a prehearing conference will be held by telephone on June 7, 2016, at 2:00 p.m.
EDT (1:00 p.m. CDT).
IT IS SO ORDERED.
