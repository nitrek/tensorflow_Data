STAY ORDER

Yesterday, the Division of Enforcement and Respondent David Prolman submitted a joint
motion to stay this proceeding as to Prolman based upon an agreement in principle to a
settlement that will resolve the proceeding as to him on all major terms.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the joint motion is GRANTED, and the
proceeding is STAYED as to Prolman, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice 161(c)(2), 17
C.F.R. ยง 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any of the requirements
of the rule are not met. All deadlines remain in effect as to the remaining parties.
