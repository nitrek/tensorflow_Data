ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings on February 12, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. The
proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based on SEC v. Schooler, No. 3:12-cv-2164 (S.D. Cal.), in
which Respondent Louis V. Schooler was enjoined against violations of the antifraud and
registration provisions. A prehearing conference was held today. Both parties appeared.
The Division was granted leave to file a motion for summary disposition pursuant to 17
C.F.R. ยง 201.250. The motion, opposition, and reply will be due April 15, May 13, and May 20,
2016, respectively. Respondent may file a motion for summary disposition according to the same
schedule, if he wishes.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
