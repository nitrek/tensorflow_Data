POSTPONEMENT ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings (OIP) on October 4, 2016, pursuant to Section 8A of the Securities Act of
1933, Section 21C of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, Sections 203(f) and 203(k) of the
Investment Advisers Act of 1940, and Sections 9(b) and 9(f) of the Investment Company Act of
1940, and the hearing was scheduled to commence on November 7, 2016.
A requested by the parties, and consistent with 17 C.F.R. § 201.161: Respondent’s
deadline to file an Answer to the OIP will be extended to November 9, 2016; oppositions and
replies to any associated motions will be November 22 and December 7, 2016, respectively; and
the hearing will be postponed sine die and a telephonic prehearing conference scheduled for
November 17, 2016, at 1:00 p.m. EST. The parties waived their right to service from each by
the means provided in 17 C.F.R. § 201.150, and will serve each other by email.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
