PREHEARING ORDER

The Securities and Exchange Commission instituted this proceeding with an Order
Instituting Proceedings (OIP) on May 13, 2016, pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. The
proceeding is a follow-on proceeding based on SEC v. Benger, No. 1:09-cv-00676 (N.D. Ill.),
appeal docketed, No. 16-1886 (7th Cir. Apr. 22, 2016), in which Respondent was enjoined
against violations of the antifraud provisions. A prehearing conference was held today. Jonathan
S. Polish and Daniel J. Hayes, Esqs., appeared for the Division of Enforcement, and James A.
McGurk, Esq., appeared for Respondent.
The parties are exploring settlement. Respondent will file his Answer to the OIP by June
27, 2016, and a second prehearing conference will be held by telephone on June 28, 2016, at 2:00
p.m. EDT (1:00 p.m. CDT). Absent settlement, the Division was granted leave to file a motion
for summary disposition pursuant to 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.250. The dates for briefing will be
established at the June 28 prehearing conference.
IT IS SO ORDERED.
