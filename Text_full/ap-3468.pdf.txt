CONFERENCE

On December 23, 2015, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an Order
Instituting Administrative Proceedings (OIP) against Respondent pursuant to Section 12(j) of the
Securities Exchange Act of 1934. The hearing is scheduled to commence on January 8, 2016.
On January 4, 2016, a declaration of service was filed stating that a process server served
the OIP on Respondent by delivering two identical copies of the OIP to the Delaware Secretary
of State on December 28, 2015. I find that Respondent was served in accordance with Rule
141(a)(2)(ii), 17 C.F.R. § 201.141(a)(2)(ii), on December 28, 2015. See 8 Del. C. § 321(b).
Respondent’s answer is due by January 7, 2016. OIP at 2; 17 C.F.R. § 201.220(b).
I ORDER that the hearing scheduled for January 8, 2016, is POSTPONED and a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 1:00 p.m. EST on January 26, 2016.
