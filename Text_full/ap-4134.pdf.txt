ORDER POSTPONING HEARING
AND SCHEDULING
PREHEARING CONFERENCE

On August 22, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. A hearing
is currently scheduled for September 13, 2016. On September 6, 2016, the Division of
Enforcement filed a motion to postpone the hearing and schedule a prehearing conference. The
motion states that the parties have reached a settlement in principle and that the Division is
preparing a motion to stay this matter pursuant to 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.161(c)(2).
I ORDER that the September 13, 2016, hearing is POSTPONED, and that a telephonic
prehearing conference shall be held on October 28, 2016, at 2:00 p.m. Eastern, if the proceeding
has not been resolved by then.
I FURTHER ORDER that if no motion to stay this matter is made by September 16,
2016, then by that date the Division shall file a declaration regarding the status of service of the
OIP on Respondent, with accompanying evidence, if any.
