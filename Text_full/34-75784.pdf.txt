On August 14, 2014, the Securities and Exchange Commission (“Commission”) issued
an order (“Order”) finding that Linkbrokers Derivatives LLC (“Respondent”) violated the federal
securities laws.1 Pursuant to the Order, Respondents paid disgorgement of $14,000,000.
The Division of Enforcement now seeks the appointment of Rust Consulting, Inc.

(“Rust”) as the fund plan administrator and requests that the administrator’s bond be set at
$14,000,000 as required by Rules 1105(a) and 1105(c) of the Commission’s Rules on Fair Fund
and Disgorgement Plans (“Rules”).2 Rust is included in the Commission’s approved pool of
administrators.
