FRANKLIN BANK CORPORATION

ORDER POSTPONING HEARING
AND SCHEDULING PREHEARING
CONFERENCE

On July 15, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, alleging that
Respondent has securities registered with the Commission and is delinquent in its periodic
filings. A hearing is scheduled to commence August 1, 2016.
I ORDER the Division of Enforcement to file by August 5, 2016, a declaration regarding
the status of service of the OIP on Respondent, with accompanying evidence, if any.
I FURTHER ORDER that the August 1, 2016, hearing is POSTPONED, and that a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held on September 23, 2016, at 2:00 p.m. Eastern, if
the proceeding has not been resolved by then.
