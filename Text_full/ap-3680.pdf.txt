In the Matter of
ORDER EXTENDING STAY
MIDDLEBURY SECURITIES, LLC

Since December 4, 2014, this proceeding has been stayed pursuant to a request by the U.S.
Attorney for the Southern District of New York. Middlebury Sec., LLC, Admin. Proc. Rulings
Release No. 2092, 2014 SEC LEXIS 4666. On March 4, 2016, the U.S. Attorney submitted a
status report, stating that the defendant in the related criminal proceeding is scheduled to be
sentenced on March 16, but that a subsequent hearing may be required if the defendant disputes
facts related to the sentencing. The U.S. Attorney therefore requests the stay be continued.
I ORDER that the stay shall remain in effect. I FURTHER ORDER that the U.S. Attorney
shall file a written notice, updating the status of the related criminal proceeding, by March 23, 2016.
