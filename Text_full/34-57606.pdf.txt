On December 7, 2004, the Commission issued its Order Approving Plan of Fund
Distribution and Appointing Administrator (Rel. No. 34-50801). The disgorgement and
prejudgment interest, and civil monetary penalty totaling $125,000.00 paid by
Respondent were held by the Commission’s Office of Financial Management in a Fair
Fund, pursuant to Section 308(a) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 (“Fair Fund”). The
Fair Fund was fully distributed on January 31, 2005, according to the terms of the Plan
of Distribution approved by the Order.
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the distribution described above is approved and
ratified as of the day of distribution, January 31, 2005.
