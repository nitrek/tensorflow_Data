On January 22, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondents pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934. The hearing is scheduled to commence on February 16, 2016.
On February 5, 2016, a declaration of service was filed stating that Respondents were
served with the OIP by February 1, 2016, in accordance with Rule 141(a)(2)(ii), 17 C.F.R.
§ 201.141(a)(2)(ii), (iv). Respondents’ answers are due by February 16, 2016. OIP at 3; 17
C.F.R. §§ 201.160(a)-(b), .220(b).
I ORDER that the hearing scheduled for February 16, 2016, is POSTPONED and a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 3:00 p.m. EST on February 29, 2016.
