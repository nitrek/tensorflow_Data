STAY ORDER AS TO LAZARE
KAPLAN INTERNATIONAL, INC.

On February 1, 2016, this office received a joint motion to stay this proceeding as to
Respondent Lazare Kaplan International, Inc., based upon Lazare’s agreement in principle with
the Division of Enforcement to a settlement that will resolve the proceeding as to Lazare on all
major terms.
For good cause shown, it is ORDERED that the joint motion is GRANTED, and the
proceeding is STAYED as to Lazare, subject to compliance with Rule of Practice 161(c)(2), 17
C.F.R. § 201.161(c)(2), requiring that this office be notified promptly if any requirements of the
Rule are not met.
