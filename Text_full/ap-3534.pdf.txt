ORDER POSTPONING
HEARING

On January 5, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order instituting
proceedings (OIP) pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and Section
203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. A hearing is currently scheduled for January 29,
2016.
Respondent is currently incarcerated and the Division of Enforcement has been unable to
contact him to find a mutually agreeable date and time for a prehearing conference. Therefore, it
is ORDERED that the hearing scheduled for January 29, 2016, is POSTPONED. The Division
shall have until February 10, 2016, to confer with Respondent and propose a date and time for
the prehearing conference. Additionally, the Division of Enforcement shall file a declaration by
February 1, 2016, regarding whether service of the OIP has been accomplished in accordance
with Rule of Practice 141, 17 C.F.R. ยง 201.141, and if so, provide proof of service.
