CONFERENCE

On September 6, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondent pursuant to Section 15(b) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934 and Section 203(f) of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. A hearing is
currently scheduled for September 30, 2016.
I ORDER the Division of Enforcement to file by October 3, 2016, a declaration regarding
the status of service of the OIP on Respondent.
I FURTHER ORDER that the September 30, 2016, hearing is POSTPONED, and that a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held on October 11, 2016, at 3:00 p.m. Eastern.
