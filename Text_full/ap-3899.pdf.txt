Since December 4, 2014, this proceeding has been stayed pursuant to a request by the U.S.
Attorney for the Southern District of New York. Gregory Osborn, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release
No. 2094, 2014 SEC LEXIS 4668. On June 3, 2016, the U.S. Attorney submitted a status report,
withdrawing its request that the stay remain in effect.
Accordingly, I LIFT the stay and ORDER that a telephonic prehearing conference take
place on Monday, June 13, 2016, at 1:30 p.m. EDT.
