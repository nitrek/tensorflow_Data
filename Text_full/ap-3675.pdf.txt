On February 25, 2016, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued an order
instituting proceedings (OIP) against Respondents pursuant to Section 12(j) of the Securities
Exchange Act of 1934. The hearing is scheduled to commence on March 23, 2016.
On March 2, 2016, the Division of Enforcement filed a declaration of service establishing
that Respondents were served with the OIP by March 1, 2016, in accordance with 17 C.F.R.
§ 201.141(a)(2)(ii). Respondents’ answers are due by March 14, 2016. OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R.
§§ 201.160(a)-(b), .220(b).
I ORDER that the hearing scheduled for March 23, 2016, is POSTPONED and a
telephonic prehearing conference shall be held at 11:00 a.m. EDT on March 28, 2016.
