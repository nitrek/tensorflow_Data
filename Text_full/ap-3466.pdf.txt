ORDER AMENDING PROCEDURAL
SCHEDULE

Following a prehearing conference held on December 15, 2015, I ordered the Division of
Enforcement to submit a letter regarding service of the Order Instituting Administrative
Proceedings (OIP) on Respondent and set a procedural schedule for motions for summary
disposition, with the Division’s opening motion (and Respondent’s cross-motion if he so
chooses) due by January 12, 2016. Brett A. Cooper, Admin. Proc. Rulings Release No. 3402,
2015 SEC LEXIS 5098. On January 4, 2016, the Division submitted its letter establishing that
Respondent was personally served with the OIP on December 26, 2015, in accordance with Rule
141(a)(2)(i), 17 C.F.R. § 141(a)(2)(i). Respondent’s answer is due by January 15, 2016. See
OIP at 3; 17 C.F.R. § 201.220(b).
In its letter, the Division requested the procedural schedule be extended so that opening
motions for summary disposition would not be due until after Respondent’s deadline to file an
answer has passed. Accordingly, the procedural schedule is AMENDED as follows:
January 26, 2016:

The Division’s motion for summary disposition is due.

February 23, 2016:

Respondent’s opposition is due.

March 4, 2016:

The Division’s reply, if any, is due.
