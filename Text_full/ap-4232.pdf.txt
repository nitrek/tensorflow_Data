On September 27, 2016, I held a telephonic prehearing conference during which a
prehearing and hearing schedule was discussed. The hearing will be held in Atlanta, Georgia, at

14, 2016, the parties should file a proposed prehearing schedule, including due dates for
amended witness and exhibit lists, stipulations, and any other filings the parties would like to
make, and a date and time for a final prehearing conference.
