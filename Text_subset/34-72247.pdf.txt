On December 12, 2013, the Commission issued an order (“Order”) finding that GLG
Partners, Inc. and GLG Partners, L.P. (“Respondents”) violated the federal securities laws. 1
Pursuant to the Order, Respondents paid disgorgement of $7,766,667, prejudgment interest of
$437,679, and civil money penalties totaling $750,000. The Order created a Fair Fund for
distribution to harmed investors pursuant to Section 308(a) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002,
as amended.
The Division of Enforcement now seeks the appointment of Boston Financial Data

Services, Inc. (“Boston Financial”) as the fund plan administrator and requests that the
administrator’s bond be set at $8,954,346, as required by Rules 1105(a) and 1105(c) of the

Accordingly, pursuant to Rule 1105(a), it is hereby ORDERED that Boston Financial is
appointed as the fund plan administrator and shall obtain a bond in accordance with Rule 1105(c)
in the amount of $8,954,346.
