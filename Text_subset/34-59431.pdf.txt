The Division of Enforcement (“Division”) seeks approval to publish the Notice of
Proposed Distribution Plan and Opportunity for Comment submitted in this matter. The
Division also seeks approval of the appointment of Gilardi & Co., LLC as plan
administrator of the proposed plan of distribution, and approval of Gilardi’s bond in the
amount of $2,500,000, the amount of the settlement paid by John W. Adams and AIP, LLC
(“Respondents”).
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Division’s request to publish the Notice of
Proposed Distribution Plan and Opportunity for Comment is granted. Furthermore, IT IS
HEREBY ORDERED that the Division’s request for approval of the appointment of Gilardi

& Co., LLC as administrator and for approval of the administrator’s bond in the amount of
$2,500,000 is granted.
