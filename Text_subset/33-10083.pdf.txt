IT IS ORDERED that this matter is scheduled for oral argument at 10:30 a.m. on Friday,
July 22, 2016, at 100 F Street, NE, Washington, DC, in the Auditorium (L-0002).
A total of 40 minutes has been allocated for the argument, 20 minutes to counsel for the
Respondents and 20 minutes to counsel for the Division of Enforcement. The parties will argue
in the above order and both sides may reserve time for rebuttal.
Rule 451 of the Commission’s Rules of Practice provides that a member of the
Commission not present at the oral argument may participate in the Commission’s decision,
subject to the understanding that he or she will review the transcript of the argument.
