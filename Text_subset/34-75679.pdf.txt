In the Matter of

ORDER DIRECTING
DISBURSEMENT OF
DISGORGEMENT FUND

On March 10, 2015, the Commission issued a Notice of Proposed Plan of Distribution and
Opportunity for Comment (“Notice”) pursuant to Rule 1103 of the Commission’s Rules on Fair
Fund and Disgorgement Plans, 17 C.F.R. § 201.1103.1 The Notice advised interested persons that
they could obtain a copy of the proposed Plan of Distribution (“Plan”) from the Commission’s
public website or by submitting a written request to Susan S. Pecaro, Esq., United States
Securities and Exchange Commission, 100 F Street, NE, Washington, DC 20549-5631. The
Notice also provided all interested persons thirty (30) days to submit a comment on the proposed
Plan. All persons who desired to comment on the proposed Plan could submit their comments, in

On April 27, 2015, the Commission issued an Order Approving Plan of Distribution.2
The Plan provides for the distribution of the Disgorgement Fund through the U.S.
Department of the Treasury’s Bureau of the Fiscal Service when the Fund Administrator submits
a payment file with payee information in a Commission-approved format. The Fund
Administrator has submitted a payment file with payee information for the disbursement of
$1,394,190.92.
Accordingly, it is ORDERED that the Commission staff shall direct the payment of
$1,394,190.92 from the Disgorgement Fund as provided for in the Plan.
