On September 13, 2013, the Securities and Exchange Commission (“Commission”)
issued an order (“Order”) finding that Sarkauskas and Associates, Inc. and James M. Sarkauskas
(“Respondents”) violated the federal securities laws.1 Pursuant to the Order, Respondents paid
disgorgement of $331,433.98, prejudgment interest of $18,403.22, and civil money penalties
totaling $100,000. The Order created a Fair Fund for distribution to harmed investors pursuant
to Section 308(a) of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002, as amended.
The Division of Enforcement now seeks the appointment of Rust Consulting, Inc.

(“Rust”) as the fund plan administrator and requests that the administrator’s bond be set at
$449,837.20, as required by Rules 1105(a) and 1105(c) of the Commission’s Rules on Fair Fund
